Featured Rafting Trips

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty Level: Expert

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on the Devil's Teeth rapids! This extreme adventure features Class V rapids that will test your skills and nerves. Not for the faint of heart, this trip is reserved for experienced rafters seeking the ultimate challenge on the water.

Duration: Full Day | Group Size: 4-8 people

Big Mallard

Difficulty Level: Intermediate

The Big Mallard route offers an exciting balance of thrilling rapids and scenic beauty. This medium-difficulty journey features Class III-IV rapids perfect for adventurers with some rafting experience. Enjoy stunning canyon views and wildlife sightings along the way.

Duration: Half Day | Group Size: 6-12 people

Multi-Day Expedition

Difficulty Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Embark on an unforgettable multi-day journey down one of the country's most magnificent river systems. This immersive experience includes camping, meals, and guided navigation through diverse rapids. Perfect for families and groups looking for a comprehensive adventure.

Duration: 3-5 Days | Group Size: 8-20 people
